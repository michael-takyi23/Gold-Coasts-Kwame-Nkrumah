Education

Kwame Nkrumah was born on 21 September 1909 in Nkroful,Gold Coast,now Ghana. At that time the so-called Gold Coast was a crown colony of Great Britain.

Kwame Nkruhmah went to a missionary school and graduated from Accra's Achimota College in 1930. With the help of a relative, Nkrumah was able to study in the United States. In 1943 he received a Master of Arts in philosophy and a Master of Science in education from the University of Pennsylvania. He taught for two years at Lincoln University.

In 1945 Kwame Nkrumah studied in London. There, his political commitment grew steadily. He organized the 5th Pan-African Congress in Manchester under the American sociologist WEB Du Bois.
